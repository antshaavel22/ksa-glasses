Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know

The procedure itself is painless — the eye is numbed with drops. After the procedure, there are 2–3 days of mild discomfort and light sensitivity, but pain is minimal. Most patients say it was much easier than they expected.
Within just minutes after the laser, vision is already significantly improved. Within the first week, vision stabilises to 80–90%. The final result arrives within 1–3 months as the cornea fully heals. Many of our Flow clients are driving confidently just one week after the procedure.
Flow3 is suitable for most short-sighted people aged 18–45. During the examination, we measure corneal thickness, eye health and dioptre stability. If Flow3 isn't right for you, we'll tell you honestly and offer alternatives. That's exactly why the exam is so important — it's your personal answer.
LASIK cuts a flap in the cornea, under which the laser works — this creates a weak point. Flow3 (TransPRK) works only on the corneal surface, without any cut or touch, reshaping the cornea by just tens of micrometres thinner and flatter. That's why the risk of ectasia is 4.5× lower and the cornea remains stronger. The US military prefers surface ablation for its pilots and special forces — that speaks for itself.
